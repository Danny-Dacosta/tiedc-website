## TIEDC - E Cell of JUIT

### What is TIEDC ?

The Technology Incubation and Entrepreneurship Development cell of JUIT aims at developing an entrepreneurial spirit and shares a vision to foster innovation and budding ideas amongst the students . The cell facilitates with a practical edge of exposing the students to hands-on experience of entrepreneurship through initiatives like Startup Simulator, E-summit, Networking Series, alumini interactions and interactive sessions with existing Entrepreneurs and Venture Capitalists.
